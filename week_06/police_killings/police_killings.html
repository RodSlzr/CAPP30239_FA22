People killed in 2015 by police officers

The dataset used for this analysis includes features sucha as gender, race, date, city, state, manner of death, if they were armed, if they have a mental illness or if they flee.


Age distribution

I chose to use this graph to see if there was a relationship between the age group and the killings. Effectively, we can see that the ages of most of the people killed ranges between 20 and 40 years old. Understanding why this groups are more vulnerable to this fatal destinies is vital to come to better solutions. Some complementary information would be the demograpgic age distribution, to disccount that effect.


Killings by race

Then, I decided to use this graph to see the amount of people killed by race. This graph can be misleading. As some people might interpret that white people is more vulnerable to be killed by the police. However, to understand the whole picture is necessary to compare it to the proportions of people of these groups. That way, we will see if some groups are more vulnerable than others.
